About Us

ZZ Hiking Group is dedicated to exploring the great outdoors and connecting with nature. We organize regular hikes and outdoor activities for enthusiasts of all levels.

ZZ Hiking Group welcomes hikers of all skill levels and backgrounds. We organize weekly guided hikes, monthly overnight trips, and occasional skill-building workshops covering navigation, Leave No Trace principles, and basic first aid. Our experienced volunteer leaders plan routes that balance challenge and safety, and we encourage participants to prepare with appropriate gear, hydration, and footwear. Beyond the trails, we foster a friendly, inclusive community through social events and environmental stewardship projects, ensuring every member feels supported and connected. Join us to explore new trails, improve your outdoor skills, and make lasting friendships in the great outdoors.
